Formulas and Examples

Height

  • 1 m = 100 cm
  • 1 foot = 12 inches = 0.3048 m
  • 1 m = 3.28084 ft
  • 1 in = 0.0254 m
  • 1 cm = 0.393701 in

Examples:

  • ► 6'1 ft to meters
  • m = 6 * 0.3048 + 1 * 0.0254
  • m = 1.8288 + 0.0254
  • m = 1.8542 ≈ 1.85
  • → 6ft 1in ≈ 1m 85cm
  • ► 1.73m to feet
  • ft = 1.73 * 3.28084
  • ft = 5.6758532 ≈ 5.675
  • Extract whole feet(5) and decimal part(0.675)
  • in = 0.675 * 12 = 8.1 ≈ 8
  • → 1m 73cm ≈ 5ft 8in

Weight

Examples

  • → 150lb ≈ 68 kg
  • ► 80 kg to lb
